Tangy cabbage slaw

A simple homemade coleslaw of traditional carrots, onion, celery and both white and red cabbage with light mustard-lemon mayonnaise

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: No

Level: easy

Let's start

Required Products:

  1. 250ml mayonnaise
  2. zest and juice 1 lemon
  3. 2 tbsp cider vinegar
  4. 2 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  5. 1 tsp celery salt
  6. ¼ head white cabbage (very thinly sliced)
  7. ¼ head red cabbage (very thinly sliced)
  8. 2 carrots (julienned)
  9. 1 large red onion (diced)
  10. 2 sticks celery (thinly sliced)

How to prepare a recipe:

Step 1: Whisk together mayonnaise, lemon zest and juice, vinegar, mustard and celery salt in a small bowl, then season generously.

Step 2: Add cabbage, carrots, onion and celery. Mix well and refrigerate. Can be made up to 1 day ahead.
